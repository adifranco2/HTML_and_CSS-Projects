Recipe for Mashed Potatoes

A simple side dish that pairs well with just about any protein.

Ingredients

5 Large Potatoes
2 T Butter
Milk
Salt & Pepper

Directions

Peel potatoes. Place potatoes in pot and fill with water. Boil potatoes until fork tender. Drain potatoes. Start mashing potatoes with potato masher or large fork. Add butter and continue mashing. Add enough milk to create creamy consistance. Add salt and pepper to taste.
